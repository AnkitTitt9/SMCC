Why Choose Us

Outsourcing

We provide global companies with reliable outsourcing solutions. Our integrity is a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence and client satisfaction.

ISO 9001

ISO 9001:2015 certified, ensuring consistent outputs and adherence to formalized business processes. Many of our top clients have been with us for more than a decade, highlighting our unwavering dedication to delivering quality services.

Security

We prioritize security at every level. SandMartin ensures an optimal level of security, encompassing both physical and network security measures. This commitment provides peace of mind to our international clients, knowing that their sensitive data is protected and conformed to compliance.

One Stop Shop

SandMartin serves as a "One Stop Shop" for all your outsourcing and consulting needs. Our extensive experience has allowed us to gain strong domain expertise in accounts and tax services, making us a scalable service provider capable of meeting your evolving outsourcing requirements.

Modern Infrastructure

Our offices in New Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, and Mumbai boast state-of-the-art IT infrastructure, delivering both physical and network security. We rely on DELL Computers for reliability and robust IT systems. Our investment ensures easy storage management, comprehensive data protection, and enterprise-class performance and reliability.

Human Resources

Our dedicated team comprises over 550+ trained professionals well-versed in Indian and international laws, taxation, accounts, and more. Our team includes CAs, CPAs, ACCAs, MBAs in Finance and Marketing, Company Secretaries, Law Graduates, Software Programmers, and Graduates.

Convenient Locations

Our offices located in the business hubs of India – Mumbai, New Delhi, Gurugram (Gurgaon) and Noida provide us an advantage to be in proximity to our client locations, enabling access to a greater talent pool enabling us to serve our clients more efficiently.

Business Continuity

With over 40 years of experience, our team of 550+ trained professionals is fully equipped to provide customized outsourcing solutions. This allows businesses to concentrate on their core activities, achieving greater organizational efficiency.

Effective Client Management System
